🦛 About Moodeng

Moodeng is an adorable pygmy hippo who has captured hearts worldwide with her playful personality and charming antics. Living at Khao Kheow Open Zoo in Thailand, she has become an internet sensation and a symbol of wildlife conservation.

🏛️ Khao Kheow Open Zoo

Located in Chonburi, Thailand, Khao Kheow Open Zoo is home to over 8,000 animals from around the world. The zoo is dedicated to wildlife conservation, education, and providing the best care for animals like Moodeng.
